The Gallery

Where art is UNVLD.

UNVLD. is the gallery arm of THE BLVD — presenting carefully curated artworks, editions and collectibles from artists shaping culture and the contemporary art market. Spanning street art, urban contemporary, modern, abstract and blue-chip works, we offer original artworks, sold-out editions, rare collectibles and exclusive releases selected for their cultural significance, collectability and long-term appeal.

While THE BLVD specialises in private sourcing, acquisitions and advisory services, UNVLD. is where exceptional works are brought into view — giving collectors direct access to pieces worth discovering, owning and living with. Headquartered between London and Dubai, we combine market expertise, industry access and genuine passion to help collectors acquire with confidence and build collections of lasting significance.
